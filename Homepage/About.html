About Us

Always Fresh-Full Of Flover

Who Says Salads Have to be Boring?

We Know That a Big Part of a Healthy Lifestyle is Finding a Balance Between What is Healthy and What is Enjoyable.

Cindy's Kitchen's mission is to stay ahead of the curve and make a wide range of innovative, clean, refrigerated salad dressings, dips, and cooking sauces that customers love. We've selected Cindy's Kitchen as our partner to create the very finest single-serve salads with dressings for both Solata Farms and Gaia Greens products. At Solata Foods, we believe that better greens and salads don't have to mean boring. Behind every Gaia Greens and Solata Farms product is our network of exceptional family farms creating the most flavorful lettuces available. We harvest, triple wash and pack, delivering to stores within just a few days so every salad is crispy, fresh and packed with nutrition, taste and goodness. Gaia Greens is our organic brand - inspired by the desire to create the supreme salad. Designed for salad-curious people - passionate about elevating the everyday salad so it's never sad, never boring and is better than you ever dreamed possible. Made with the freshest, brilliantly flavored organic greens, we inspire you to create salads that are truly crave-worthy! Gaia Greens sources exclusively from premium quality organic farms, delivering product quickly to market. Better greens make a better salad so only the freshest, sparkling flavors ever reach your fork. Tender and delicious, Gaia Greens are organic, clean label, with no artificial anything. Always fresh and full of flavor, Gaia Greens offers inspired combinations of clean-label, organic salads in packaged greens, pre-made kits, and single serve so every salad is something you crave.

Meanwhile, Solata Farms is our conventional brand with over 35 years in the leafy greens industry. We source high-quality greens and lettuces from family farms across the US. We are meticulous in producing Solata Farms products, visually inspecting every bunch of lettuce so only the freshest, most flavorful and tender leaves reach the store...and your plate. Every Solata Farms product is triple-washed and packed in the Hudson Valley, New York, with every product meeting our highest standard of food safety. With decades of experience in fresh greens sourcing, packing and delivery, Solata Foods is your destination for the freshest lettuces, pre-made salads and kits available.
