Food Safety

Food Safety at Solata Foods.

Packing, safe, nutritious food for our customers is the core of what we do. We strive to achieve our core values of freshness, food safety, and customer value every day. To achieve this we carefully select our suppliers and visit their farms to ensure they are meeting all of the standards to which we hold them. Our packing facility is subject to rigorous third-party audits that inspect, cleanliness, record keeping, and traceability. So we can guarantee our salads are safe to eat.

Lori Ward is in charge of our food safety and quality program at Solata Foods.
She is responsible for day-to-day oversight of the plant. Her attention to detail ensures that only the finest, safest salads leave our facility.
